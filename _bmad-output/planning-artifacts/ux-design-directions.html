Layout

Three-column: Watchlist left, chart+portfolio center, chat right. Trade bar docked in watchlist panel. Most similar to TradingView.

Strengths

Trade-offs

Layout

Wide left watchlist (320px) with full ticker rows showing symbol, name, price, change %, and sparkline in a scannable table format. All 10 tickers visible at once.

Strengths

Best information density in the watchlist. All tickers readable at a glance without scrolling. Wide format suits the Bloomberg "screener" mental model. Great for market monitoring.

Trade-offs

Less space for chart and chat. Right column feels compressed. Chart height is limited. Best suited for users who watch the whole market more than individual charts.

Direction Evaluation Guide

Best for showcasing AI

→ Dir 03 (Command Center) or Dir 01 (Classic). Wide chat panel keeps AI front and center.

Most Bloomberg-like

→ Dir 04 (Four-Quadrant). Every panel visible simultaneously. Maximum density. Most impressive at first glance.

Best chart experience

→ Dir 02 (Chart Dominant) or Dir 05 (Minimal). Chart gets maximum vertical space.

Best watchlist scanability

→ Dir 06 (Wide Watchlist). All 10 tickers visible with full details in a Bloomberg-style screener format.

Most balanced / recommended

→ Dir 01 (Classic Terminal). Balanced three panels. Familiar. All features visible. Closest to the PLAN.md spec.

Easiest to implement

→ Dir 05 (Minimal). Simplest layout. Fewest edge cases. Fastest to implement correctly.
